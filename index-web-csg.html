FAQ

Common
questions.

Do we need to be physically present in Morocco?

No. CSG acts as your commercial and legal representative in Morocco. You do not need to open a local office or be physically present to bid on public tenders.

How long does it take to win a first tender?

Typically 3–6 months from our first engagement. This includes market analysis, documentation preparation, and submission. Timeline depends on sector and tender cycle.

What is your fee structure?

We work on a retainer plus success fee model. The retainer covers market analysis and ongoing management. The success fee aligns our interests with yours — we win when you win.

Do you work with all company sizes?

Yes, from SMEs to large corporations. We tailor our approach to your size and budget. SMEs often benefit most since we give them capabilities they could not build internally.

Which languages are tenders published in?

Moroccan public tenders are published in Arabic and French. We handle all documentation in both languages, so you never need to worry about language barriers.

Can you help with import and logistics?

Yes. Once a tender is awarded, we manage the full import process including customs clearance, freight forwarding and last-mile delivery to the contracting institution.
